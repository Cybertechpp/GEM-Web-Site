					<h2>About GEM</h2>
					<div class="h-5"></div>
					<p class="f4">GEM is a gift-based community network that supports youth’s service projects and personal development. The community offers resources to the youth and the youth utilize those gifts to pay it forward to the community and the earth. This cycle uplifts young people, builds community and helps heal the planet.</p>
					<div class="h-25"></div>
					<p class="f4">Youth (ages 12-24) who join the network are invited to create a service project that expresses their innovative solutions for change, and utilize our next-level crowdfunding platform to obtain resources for their project. The GEM Projects pillar is a space for youth to showcase their projects with video, photos and news updates. This provides an opportunity to become a featured project, connect with like-minded peers who want to join their cause, and inspire the community to support their mission.</p>
					<div class="h-25"></div>
					<p class="f4">Individuals, organizations and eco-­friendly companies who join the network are being invited to contribute to youth’s service projects and personal development by offering time, services, money or product donations. This integrative model ensures that young people who join the network will receive an abundance of opportunities and resources to support them as they set out to create positive change in their local and global community.</p>
					<div class="h-25"></div>
					<p class="f4">Everyone who joins the GEM Global Network will discover an abundance of opportunities to connect, collaborate and grow. This is a space to feature your creative solutions, develop your projects, receive inspiration and become a part of a growing movement. </P>
